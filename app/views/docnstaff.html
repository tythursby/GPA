Doctors & Staff

Thomas A. Berens, DPM originally from Wausau Wisconsin, he graduated University of Florida with an undergraduate degree in chemistry before attending the Ohio College of Podiatric Medicine in 1984. Dr. Berens completed a podiatric surgical residency program at Southeastern Medical Center in North Miami Beach Florida in 1986. He has been in private practice in Gainesville since 1986 and started Gainesville Podiatry Associates at that time. Dr. Berens is board certified in reconstructive foot and ankle surgery by the American Board of Podiatric Surgery and is a Fellow with the American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons. He has participated in the profession on multiple state and national committees and has published several articles on foot and ankle surgery.

John R. Heiser, DPM graduated from the University of Pittsburgh with an undergraduate degree in Biology before attempting the Ohio College of Podiatric Medicine in 1984. He completed a podiatric surgical residency at the Podiatry Hospital of Pittsburgh in Pittsburgh Pennsylvania in 1985. He started a private practice in Pittsburgh before moving to Gainesville to join Gainesville Podiatry Associates in 1992. Dr. Heiser is board certified in Foot Surgery by the American Board of podiatric surgery and is a Fellow with the American College and Ankle Surgeons and a Fellow with the American Board of Primary Podiatric Medicine. He has been an avid runner starting in high school and has completed multiple marathons including the Boston Marathon.
