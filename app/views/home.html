Office Hours:

M - F: 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM

Patients are seen in the office by appointment. The answering service begins taking all calls after 5:00 PM.

Office Visits:

Anyone with an urgent concern will be seen as soon as possible; simply make an appointment through the receptionist. Please do not walk in without an appointment, as an additional fee may be charged. If a medical emergency arises, please call the office so we can adjust the schedule and assemble any necessary supplies before your arrival.

Established in 1986, Gainesville Podiatry Associates provides comprehensive care of the foot and ankle for patients of all ages. From infants to the most seasoned of our citizens, we strive to address each of your concerns. Realizing each person has different needs, we offer a personalized approach to your care.
