Medical Services and Conditions

Our doctors are skilled at treating most conditions of the foot and ankle.
Some of the more common conditions that we manage include:

  • Bunions ( Hallux Valgus )
  • Hammertoe Deformities
  • Achilles Tendon Problems
  • Ingrown Nails
  • Plantar Warts
  • Sprains and Strains
  • Fractures Throughout the Foot
  • Heel Pain ( Plantar Fasciitis )
  • Tendinitis
  • Pediatric Deformities
  • Arch Pain and Flat Feet
  • Bone Spurs and other Bone Problems
  • Lumps and Soft Tissue Masses of the Foot and Ankle
  • Neuroma and Nerve Pain of the Foot and Ankle
  • Diabetic Foot Conditions
  • Sports Injuries for the Young & not so Young Athlete !
Inpatient Hospital Consultations are also available at North Florida Regional Medical Center on a rotating basis with other podiatry practices.

* Special requests for inpatient consultations on weeks other than our scheduled call may be considered if you are an existing patient.

Emergency Room Care

Emergency room care is provided on a rotating basis through North Florida Regional Medical Center. If you have a need to be seen in the of emergency room you may request to see one of our doctors if they are available.

Active Ankle provides products for both injury prevention and post injury care. This leader in foot and ankle care products provides quality devices with comfortable fit and function. Ask you doctor if an Active Ankle product may be beneficial to you recovery or prevention of an injury.


Orthotic devices are very affective in managing a variety biomechanical foot pathologies. Orthotics come in many shapes and styles with prices ranging greatly. Your doctor at Gainesville Podiatry Associates will be happy to discuss orthotic options with you looking to fine the most appropriate device for both your treatment and your pocketbook. Occasionally a custom orthotic is necessary but in many other cases a device such as the 1st Step orthotic from Wrymark can be modified just for you. We also offer a midrange leather device which may be more durable.
